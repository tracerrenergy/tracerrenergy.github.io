Three product pillars

Pillar 1 — Cross-entity validation, audit, and signoff

Ingest and reconcile settlement data across asset owners, optimisers, suppliers, and TSOs; run checks and review flows; produce transparent, auditable signoff.

Pillar 2 — TSO settlement rules as APIs

Provide market-specific settlement logic (imbalance, FCR, aFRR, mFRR, penalties, etc.) via APIs for trading, algo, and settlement teams, reducing implementation burden and costly assumption errors.

Pillar 3 — Settlement workflow framework (portfolio to asset)

Model expected revenues at portfolio and asset level, reconcile TSO portfolio-level settlement, allocate corrections down to assets, and support a clear audit trail plus structured dispute handling.

Combined value proposition

Tracerr helps teams settle accurately, explain outcomes clearly, avoid delayed penalty or fee surprises, and scale into new markets faster with lower operational and implementation risk.
